### These procedure steps will be followed on the simulator

1. Open the simulation and go through the Objective and Description, then click the 'NEXT' button in the bottom right corner.<br>
<img src="experiment/images/page1.png"/>

2. Select one of the two options: either "Alphabets" or "Figure Code." Then, click on any option within the table.<br>
<img src="experiment/images/page2.png"/>

3. Get the phonetic equivalent of the selected alphabet or figure code, then click the 'Pronounce' button to see and hear how it sounds. To repeat the process for another alphabet or figure code, click the "Back" button.<br>
<img src="experiment/images/page2.1.png"/>
